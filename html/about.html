About Us

We just wanted to make good durum wheat pasta. So in 1979, we decided to create a company which would bring the excellence and incomparable high quality of Italian food all over the world. Our hard work is inspired by constant dedication and passion is the key to our success. Today, we take pride in bringing to the tables of millions of people only the best of the Italian food tradition, the excellence and undisputed benefits of the Mediterranean Diet.
